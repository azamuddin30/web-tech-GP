What is mental health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological,
and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel,
and act as we cope with life. It also helps determine
how we handle stress, relate to others, and make
choices. Mental health is important at every stage of
life, from childhood and adolescence through
adulthood and aging.

Find your way to your stronger self

Why is mental health important?

Mental health is important because it can help you to

- Cope with the stresses of life
- Be physically healthy
- Have good relationships
- Make meaningful contributions to tour community
- Work productively
- Realize your full potential

Statistics

Research shows that mental illnesses are common in the world, affecting tens of millions of people each year. Estimates suggest that only half of people with mental illnesses receive treatment. The information on these pages includes currently available statistics on the prevalnce and treatment of mental illnesses in the world. In addition, information is provided about possible consequences of mental illnesses, such as suicide and disability.
