Our Services

Heartminded Therapy offers counselling session for those who seek help to get better. We provide two types of services, one-to-one therapy and group therapy. People can choose whichever therapy they prefer and comfortable with. One of our policy is to make sure every session confidential. All of the problems shared will not be disclosed to anyone. This is to protect the privacy of every individuals that seek help from Heartminded Therapy. Our top priority is to help people in need by providing counseling sessions for them. They can make an appointment with our specialist to talk more about their problems. To know more about services provided, click the link below.

One-to-One Session | Group session

Types of Services

One-to-One Session
One-to-one session is a personal counseling between an individual and counselor. One-to-one session focused on helping individual who seeks professional help. During this therapy, our therapist will let the individual to open up on their problems.

Group Session
Group session is conducted in a group of people. The benefit of group session is that it help them to realized that they are not alone in the journey to be better from mental health issues. Individual may choose to group among their acquaintance or they can choose to join group among strangers.
